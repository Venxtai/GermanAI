AI Tools for Impuls Deutsch

Created by Niko Tracksdorf, Herausgeber and co-author of the Impuls Deutsch textbook series, these AI-powered tools help teachers and students get more out of the curriculum.

These tools are independent projects, not officially part of the Impuls Deutsch textbook scope, and have no association with Klett World Languages. They are currently available to prototype testers and research collaborators only.

Conversation Buddy

Practice speaking German with an AI conversation partner that adapts to your curriculum level. Vocabulary and grammar are aligned with the specific Impuls Deutsch units you've covered in class.

Start Practicing →

Text Analyzer

Analyze German texts against your students' vocabulary and grammar level. Get word-by-word color coding, replacement suggestions, auto-adaptation, and export to PDF for student or teacher use.
