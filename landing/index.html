AI Tools for Impuls Deutsch

Created by Niko Tracksdorf, Herausgeber and co-author of the Impuls Deutsch textbook series, these AI-powered tools help teachers and students get more out of the curriculum. The tools are independent projects, not officially part of the Impuls Deutsch textbook series, and have no association with Klett World Languages. They are currently available to prototype testers and research collaborators only.

Conversation Buddy

Students can practice their interpersonal speaking skills in German with the Impuls Deutsch Conversation Buddy, which adapts to the curriculum. Vocabulary and grammar are aligned with exactly what has been covered in class, and conversations focus on topics students are familiar with or interested in. Instructors can easily create student access codes with flexible topic selection or assign specific chapter topics with deadlines. At the end, students receive detailed feedback on what they were able to do with the language, based on curriculum-aligned Can-Do statements.

Text Analyzer

Instructors who want to include supplementary materials or design additional assessments can now ensure that texts, worksheets, or exam questions stay within the vocabulary and grammar their students have learned. The Text Analyzer checks each text against the Impuls Deutsch curriculum and provides a readability score with word-by-word color coding, replacement suggestions, sentence rewriting, auto-adaptation, word lookup, batch comparison of multiple texts, and export to PDF for both student and teacher use.
